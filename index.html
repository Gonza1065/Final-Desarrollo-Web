History

Justin Drew Bieber is a Canadian singer-songwriter. In 2008, the former executive of the music industry, Scooter Braun, accidentally discovered the talent of a very young Justin Bieber when he was watching some videos on YouTube, where Justin was uploading some versions of his favorite singers that he performed with his guitar. Some time later, Scooter Braun became his manager. Scooter Braun met Justin Bieber with fellow American singer Usher in Atlanta, United States, where Justin would sign a contract with Scooter Braun and Usher's record label, Raymond Braun Media Group.
